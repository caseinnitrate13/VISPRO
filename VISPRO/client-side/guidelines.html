User Guidelines

Content Providers play a crucial role in the uploading of media content on the digital signage and display system. To qualify as a Content Provider for VisPro, individuals must meet the following requirements:

Content Providers should possess prior experience in using technologies, particularly desktop software and websites.

Content Providers must demonstrate an understanding of local regulations and copyright issues, along with knowledge of ADA (American with Disabilities Act) requirements and guidelines.

Content Providers are required to have prior knowledge and experience in Content Management.

Having background in advertising is encouraged for Content Providers.

Content Providers should be willing to undergo training specifically tailored to efficiently use the VisPro system.

Content Providers should be aware of the branding and messaging objectives of the Local Government Unit (LGU) and San Vicente businesses.
Content Guidelines

Use the recommended resolution of 1920 x 1080 pixels and the standard aspect ratio of 16:9.

Contrast: Use text with sufficiently high contrast with the background to be legible to those with limited vision.

Font Size: Keep text large enough to be readable from a reasonable distance. For example, use font sizes between 10-20 points for text that needs to be read from 10-20 feet away, and larger fonts for closer viewing distances.

Text Clarity: Use straightforward terms and sentence structure. Be as brief as possible to ensure clarity and conciseness. Supplement text with clear, simple graphics where necessary.

Message Hierarchy: Apply an information hierarchy with a large heading, smaller copy, and a medium-sized call-to-action (CTA) to guide the viewer's attention.

File Formats: Submit your files in JPG or PNG format for individual promotional images.

Video Guidelines: Use a 16:9 aspect ratio for videos. Videos with dialogue must include closed captioning, and do not use fast, flashing, or strobing elements.
